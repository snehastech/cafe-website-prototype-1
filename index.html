About CAFE AMADO

Where passion meets perfection in every cup and bite

Our Story

CAFE AMADO was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and artisan food. Located in the heart of Arera Colony, Bhopal, we've created a space where quality meets comfort, and every visit feels like coming home.

Our specialty coffee is sourced from the finest beans and crafted by expert baristas. Our sourdough bakery produces fresh bread daily, and our brick oven pizzas are made with authentic techniques and premium ingredients.
